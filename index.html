About Business

"H2GO Power provides a safe method of hydrogen storage in order to generate power on the move."

Hydrogen Storage Solution

H2GO Power has developed a safe method for hydrogen production and storage to provide power on the move. Our hydrogen acts as a fuel feedstock for fuel cells, clean high efficiency energy storage devices.

Material Design

We are a spin-out company from the Department of Chemistry, University of Cambridge (UK). Our core technology involves a novel hybrid smart material, which produces, stores and releases hydrogen gas in a controlled manner.

Platform Technology

Our unique solution allows for fuel cell manufacturers to deploy fuel cells into new markets such as portable off-grid electronics. The potential outcome of our technology includes a power supply with 5 times the energy capacity of current battery technology.

The Hydrogen Sponge

H2GO Power's patented technology involves a highly porous nano-particle-based smart material that combines production, storage and controlled release of hydrogen. This three feature approach allows for enhanced safety and complete fuel utilisation. The catalytic properties of the material allow for hydrogen production through the hydrolysis of metal and chemical hydrides at room temperature. The produced hydrogen is then trapped within the polymer framework, which acts as a storage medium. The stored hydrogen is released through the application of heat, with hydrogen release occurring at a specific tuneable temperature allowing for on-off control. Click here for a more detailed explanation.

Bespoke Cartridge Design

The produced hydrogen can then be fed into a fuel cell which converts hydrogen's chemical energy into electrical power. Existing systems involve the fuel cell part and a hydrogen providing part, which can be combined together resulting in a complete device. The hydrogen providing part needs to meet certain engineering parameters to meet performance and safety requirements.

At H2GO Power we design our hydrogen storage system in an application specific manner based on customers specifications such as performance, cost and safety.

Enhanced Capacity

More hydrogen can be packed into our material, meaning our 'battery' will last 5 times longer than current lithium-ion tech.

Low Pressure

The solid-state hydrogen storage approach eliminates the need for dangerous high pressures.

Lightweight

We only use lightweight elements and no bulk metal, meaning our solution is only a fraction of the weight of existing products.

Low Temperature

The reduced release temperature of hydrogen results in more hydrogen being available to power your devices.

Scaleable

The nano-scale design of our material means that it works all the way from the smallest to the largest applications.

Safer

The unique on-off control of hydrogen release translates to a much safer storage system and less engineering controls.
